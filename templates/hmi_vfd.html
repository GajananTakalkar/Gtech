HMI Solutions

Our Expertise

At GTech Automation, we offer cutting-edge HMI (Human Machine Interface) solutions that simplify machine operations and improve process visibility. Our intuitive interfaces enable operators to interact efficiently with automation systems in real time.

Services Include:

  • HMI Screen Design & Development – User-friendly, graphical interfaces for real-time interaction.
  • Integration with PLC/SCADA Systems – Seamless data communication for complete control.
  • Custom Dashboards & Visualization – Tailored layouts and alerts based on industry needs.
  • Touch Panel & Industrial Display Configuration – Installation and support for HMIs across all brands.

Technologies We Use

We work with popular HMI platforms such as:

  • Siemens HMI (WinCC)
  • Allen-Bradley PanelView (FactoryTalk)
  • Delta DOP Series
  • Mitsubishi GOT
  • Weintek, Schneider, Pro-Face
  • And other major OEMs

VFD Solutions

Our Expertise

Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) are key to modern motor control, and GTech Automation provides full-service VFD solutions—from selection and installation to programming and energy optimization—for a wide range of applications.

Services Include:

  • VFD Selection & Installation – Choosing the right drive for your motor and load requirements.
  • Drive Programming & Tuning – Customized logic, speed control, and safety features.
  • Integration with Automation Systems – Seamless communication with PLCs and SCADA systems.
  • Energy Efficiency & Maintenance – Reducing power usage and ensuring long-term performance.

Technologies We Use

We specialize in VFDs from industry-leading manufacturers:

  • ABB
  • Siemens
  • Allen-Bradley (PowerFlex Series)
  • Delta
  • Mitsubishi
  • Danfoss, Schneider Electric, and more
